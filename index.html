About

Luqks's first album is Half Here, encapsulating the feeling of drifting off, or just not fully being in the moment. It encorporates the feeling of memory and what's to come. He has plans to contribute to Tomorrowland in the future while managing the different genres.

buoyant

Buoyant is a piece that made me think of how you can just float above all your problems, without a care in the world you can live your life the way you want to.

surge

Surge is a the power piece, I just saw the pulse would just drive you into space, like for some it would send them off track, however no matter how many pulses they throw, the song plays.

submerge

Submerge is a piece that holds tenseness. Something very uneasy and I myself find underwater places very unsettling, leaving that piece to remind me of that.

back

together

Together is the love behind this piece, I love how nostalgic it sounds, almost like a lullaby, but has this unexplainable warmth.

back

wander

Wander is the piece I chose to demonstrate exploring. Exploring is never easy, as you would have to go around to uncharted territory, or discover new things, it always had this distant tone, but this piano feels different...

back

wet circuit

Wet Circuit has all these chords and trills to it with many SFX/ear candy to it, making it feel more dystopian than it should. Like a nights drive in a road you've never been to before.

back

sightseer's lullaby

Sightseer's Lullaby is a lullaby that I thought would sleep to. This song shows the adventure from uncertainty to the highs and lows of any trip.

september

September is my first piece of this album. If you are reading this, it was originally called "Distant", then later on "Distant2" as this was the 2nd model of the song. It has been many close friends and family's favourites of the bunch and I do hope it's yours too.
