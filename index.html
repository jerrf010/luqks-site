About

Luqks's debut album Half Here encapsulates the feeling of drifting off — of not being fully present in a moment. It weaves together themes of memory and anticipation, longing and surrender. With plans to contribute to Tomorrowland and an ever-expanding range of genres, Luqks is only just beginning.
